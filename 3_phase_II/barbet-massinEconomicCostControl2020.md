## Other Quantitative Data

### Study Details
- **Data collection**: 10 administrations (2 departments, 8 cities) with nest destruction cost data
- **Cost period (France)**: 2006-2015
- **Total cost (France, 2006-2015)**: €23 million
- **Yearly cost trend**: Increasing as species spreads
- **Projected yearly costs** (if species fills climatically suitable distribution):
  - France: €11.9 million
  - Italy: €9.0 million
  - United Kingdom: €8.6 million
- **Cost predictor**: Human population size found to be strong predictor
- **Spatial extrapolation**: Adjusted by per capita GDP (PPP) for other countries

### Invasion History
- **France introduction**: 2004 (south-western France)
- **Spread rate (France)**: 60-80 km/year
- **Other countries**: Spain (2010), Portugal and Belgium (2011), Italy (2012), Germany (2014), UK (2016)
- **Presence records (Europe)**: 10,395 records from 2004
- **Climate suitability modelling**: 8 modelling techniques, ensemble forecast

### Control Methods
- **Primary method**: Nest destruction
- **Other methods**: Bait trapping (limited efficiency)
- **Strategy**: Early identification and systematic nest destruction
