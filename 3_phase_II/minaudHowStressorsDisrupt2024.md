### Winter Bee Characteristics
- **Lifespan**: 130 days average (vs. 30 days for spring/summer bees)
- **Vitellogenin levels**: Higher in winter bees
- **Immune system**: Enhanced (increased defense proteins, better hemocyte viability, phagocytic response)
- **Flight capacity**: Reduced
- **Respiration capacity**: Reduced
- **Hypopharyngeal glands**: Reduced activity

### Social Thermoregulation
- **Temperature threshold**: Below 15°C
- **Core temperature**: Average 21.3°C (center: 27-35°C, decreasing to 18-29°C at surface)
- **Mantle temperature**: 10-15°C (never below 6.1°C)
- **Heat-generating bees**: 36% at 14°C, 15% at 19°C
- **Surface reduction**: >88% compared to total surface of all individuals
- **Minimum temperature**: Can withstand -80°C for up to 1 hour (laboratory)
- **Relative humidity**: Stabilized around 60%
- **Oxygen**: Stabilized around 15%

### Colony Requirements
- **Minimum weight (autumn)**: 20 kg (excluding wooden materials)
- **Minimum population**: 4,000-5,000 bees
- **Daily weight loss (winter)**: 39 ± 13 g per day
- **Consumption per individual**: Higher in smaller colonies

### Cascading Effects
- **Studies showing cascading effects**: 21 studies (28.4%)
- **Key integrative traits**: Population size, social thermoregulation, honey reserves

## Other Quantitative Data

### Literature Search Details
- **Database**: Web of Science (WOS) section of ISI Web of Knowledge
- **Function used**: Topics function (TS)
- **Exclusion**: Survey articles filtered by title (TI)
- **Selection criteria**:
  1. Original scientific article in English on honey bee biology
  2. Study conducted during winter or on winter bees
  3. Showing significant effect of stress factor on biological trait
  4. (Fourth criterion not specified in abstract)

### Feedback Loop Mechanism
- **Concept**: Premature mortality → reduced population → distorted cluster → disrupted thermoregulation → over-consumption → starvation → further mortality

### Monitoring Tools
- **Temperature sensors**: 1 to >60 sensors per frame
- **Thermal cameras**: Less accurate, cannot detect cluster location
- **Connected scales**: Common tool in precision beekeeping
- **Cost threshold**: 200 euros per colony (affordability threshold)

### Geographic Context
- **Winter mortality rates**: 25-50% in Europe
- **Beekeeping latitude**: Up to 67° north (Norway)

### Treatment Efficiency
- **Varroa treatment**: >25% reduction if recommendations not followed
- **Indoor overwintering**: Can improve physiology, population, decrease consumption
