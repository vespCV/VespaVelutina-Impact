### Stressors Mentioned
- **Five main stressors** for global declines in managed honeybee colonies:
  1. Human-driven spread of pathogenic and pest organisms (Varroa destructor, Aethina tumida, Vespa velutina)
  2. Landscape changes (habitat loss/degradation)
  3. Intensification of agricultural production (fertilizers, pesticides)
  4. Climate change and weather intensification
  5. Recent invasions of new, non-native plant species
